Z E I T F O R M E N
das Präsens    - about present & future                                     ich lerne
das Präteritum - stories & reports, mostly when writing                     ich lernte
das Futur I    - to express intention or suppostion                         ich werde lernen
das Perfekt    - focuses on the result of sth that took place in the past   ich habe gelernt
das Plusquamperfekt - sth that happened before another action in the past   ich hatte gelernt
das Futur II   - an assumption that by the time of speaking,                ich werde gelernt haben
                   or atlater point, an action will have been completed
